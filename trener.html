Trener og instruksjon

Marc Duaso jobber hos oss som padel trener. Marc er klar for både privat instruksjon, gruppetreninger og bedriftsarrangement. Marc kan nåes direkte på 919 02 490 eller marcsantinduaso@gmail.com.

En liten introduksjon fra Marc selv:

"I am Marc Santin Duaso, a padel coach and player. I've been living in Oslo for the last five years working as a tennis coach on different projects. I come from Spain where padel has become the most popular sport during the last 15 years and where there are many professional padel players. I am happy to be part of Fornebu Padel's team and am looking forward to teaching you this wonderful sport."
